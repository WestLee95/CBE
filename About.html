Our Company

CBE Limited was established in 2008 and incorporated in 2015. We have grown tremendously through continuous passion in offering engineering solutions and experience to our clients. Headquartered in Kitengela, Kenya, with branches in Mombasa, Kisumu, Eldoret, and Nakuru, we provide comprehensive support for downstream petroleum facilities.

Our Core Business

  • Installation, maintenance, and repair of petroleum dispensing equipment.
  • Calibration and maintenance of underground tanks.
  • Electrical and electronic engineering services.
  • Repair and maintenance of fuel dispensers.

Why Choose CBE Limited

Our commitment to customer satisfaction and continuous innovation sets us apart. We are dedicated to delivering reliable services with technical expertise, ensuring a win-win partnership with every client.
